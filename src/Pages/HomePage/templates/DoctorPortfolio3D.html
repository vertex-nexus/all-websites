About Dr. Sharma

With over a decade of experience in internal medicine, Dr. Arin Sharma is a board-certified physician dedicated to holistic patient wellness. A graduate of the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), he has built a reputation for his meticulous approach to diagnostics and his empathetic patient care. Dr. Sharma is also a published researcher with a focus on metabolic disorders and preventative medicine, constantly seeking to integrate the latest scientific findings into his clinical practice.
