Our Hundred-Year Journey

Building upon a foundation of trust since 1925.

1925

Founded by Arjun Sharma, supplying stone and timber for local infrastructure.

1968

Expanded into heavy machinery leasing, acquiring our first fleet of excavators.

1995

Pioneered the supply of modern power tools and electronic surveying equipment in the region.

2025

Leading the charge in sustainable building materials and celebrating 100 years of service.
